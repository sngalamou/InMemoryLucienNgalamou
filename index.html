On the fateful day of August 19th, 2023, after a heartfelt session of worship in church, Dr. Ngalamou's journey on Earth was tragically cut short. He was on his way home in Minooka, IL, when an unforeseen accident struck, taking him away from those who held him dear.

His son, Simeon Ngalamou, alongside his brothers Emmanuel and Mathieu, now bear the solemn duty of ensuring their father's last wishes are honored. The family is working together to navigate the complex responsibilities that follow such a sudden loss. This includes preparing a service in Illinois and ensuring Dr. Ngalamou's final rest beside his late parents in his ancestral home, Bafang, Cameroon. Moreover, they are faced with the responsibility of managing the estate of a man who was a pillar to so many, requiring them to engage with legal processes and professionals to gather essential details about their father.

As the Ngalamou family contends with this immense loss, they express deep gratitude for the outpouring of love, support, and generosity from the community. Dr. Lucien Ngalamou's memory is an ember that will forever glow warmly in the hearts of all who knew him.
